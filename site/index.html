Politics begins when real virtues collide.

The Political Prism does not ask whether you are left or right. It maps the instinct you trust when Freedom, Equality, and Stability pull politics in different directions.

The three moral axes

The model is a triangle because every answer moves toward one sacrifice.

01

Equality

Standing against domination. It asks whether people meet as moral equals or as masters and dependents.

02

Freedom

Agency against absorption. It asks what the individual can own, say, choose, trade, refuse, and exit.

03

Stability

Continuity against collapse. It asks what a society can preserve, coordinate, and become across time.
